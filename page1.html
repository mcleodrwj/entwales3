Public Transport
For visitors arriving at Cardiff Central Station, the distance between the station and University Hospital Landough is approximately 4 miles. 
There is a Cardiff Bus service (see below) that goes to the hospital.   A journey from the station by taxi should take approximately 15 minutes.   

Bus Services
The most direct Cardiff to UHL service is the Cardiff Bus no 95/51/53.  The nearest bus stop is on Penlan Road by the main hospital gate. 
The following services are also available: Services 95/51/53 - provide UHW / UHL through services, via Cardiff Central Bus Station, on selected daytime journeys.

Train Services

Cogan 1.3 miles Eastbrook 1.2 miles
